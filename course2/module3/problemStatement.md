[cito](https://www.coursera.org/learn/start-ux-design-process/supplement/DDRka/define-problem-statements)
"Identifying Pain Points

Pain points are UX issues that frustrate users and hinder their experience.
Understanding these pain points helps designers create solutions that enhance user satisfaction.

the 5 Ws and H Framework

The 5 Ws and H (who, what, when, where, why, how) framework aids in defining user problems.
This framework helps clarify the context of the user's experience and the significance of their pain points.

Creating Problem Statements

A well-structured problem statement includes the user’s characteristics, needs, and insights.
Problem statements guide designers in empathizing with users and developing effective solutions."
