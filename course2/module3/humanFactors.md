## psychology principles that influence design

1. Von Restorff effect
(Isolation effect)
When multiple, similar objects are present, the one that differs from the rest is most likely to be remembered

thats why call to action buttones tends to differenciate.

2. Serial position effect
When people are given a list of Ã­tems, they are more likely to remember the first few and the last few, while the items in the middle tend to blur

that's why in social media, the most important items tend to be in the start o end of an array. Example, the homebar on instagram, top left is publications, top right my profile.

3. Hick's Law

The more options a user has the longer it takes for them to make a decision

thats why you want to give little options at a time
